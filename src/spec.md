# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a comprehensive professional website for Aparna Natural Millets Producer Co. Ltd., a farmer-owned millet processing cooperative in Rayagada, Odisha, showcasing their 1 TPD processing unit project, mission, technical capabilities, and social impact.

**Planned changes:**
- Create Hero section with company name, tagline "Powered by Farmers. Trusted by Families.", and headline about the 1 TPD Millet Processing Unit project
- Build About the Company section explaining the farmer-owned cooperative model, alignment with Odisha Millets Mission (OMM) and PMFME scheme
- Create Project Vision & Core Objectives section with three objectives: Assured Procurement, Post-harvest Loss Reduction, and Nutrition & Supply
- Build Why Rayagada section explaining local context, market challenges, local processing benefits, and projected social impact (500+ farmers)
- Create Technical Specifications section showcasing the 1 TPD processing design with four components: Pre-cleaning & Grading, De-huller & Destoner, Pulverizer, and Roaster & Automatic Packaging
- Build Financial Overview section presenting Total Project Cost (₹1.15 Crore), funding breakdown, and Year 1 projections (Revenue: ₹1.50 Crore, Net Profit: ₹35 Lakhs)
- Create Social & Economic Impact section highlighting Employment & Inclusion (15 jobs), Farmer Livelihoods (500+ members), and Nutrition & Food Security
- Build Market Strategy section with three channels: B2G (Institutional), B2B (Retail & Mission Outlets), and B2C (Brand 'Aparna Millets')
- Create Value Chain Process section illustrating five stages with operational KPI targets for Year 1
- Build Support Requested section with four specific requests for government and district administration
- Create Conclusion & Next Steps section outlining three phases from commissioning (0-3 months) to scale & consolidate (9-24 months)
- Build Contact section with contact information for Bibhuti Bhusana Mishra and interactive contact form
- Implement professional visual design with warm, earthy tones reflecting agricultural heritage and cooperative values
- Create responsive navigation system for all major sections with mobile-responsive layout

**User-visible outcome:** A complete, professional website that government officials, investors, and potential customers can use to learn about Aparna Natural Millets Producer Co. Ltd., understand their processing capabilities, view financial projections, see social impact, explore market strategy, and submit inquiries through a contact form.
